Swaroopa Sales

Identity design for an architectural metal products retailer

Swaroopa Sales sells architectural metal rails, grills and decorative designs. It also sells fittings and fixtures. In short, they provide accurately engineered products and artistically designed decor items all under the same roof.

And we had to showcase both these sides in their new brand identity with a prickly rose plant shaped like 'S' in the background and bold sharp black cut circles that form 'S' containing the red surrounding the rose.

"Red and Black. Artistic and Accurate."

We also created a business card for them that stood out from the rest and gained instant recognition. Also we created a banner design in the local language using devnagri fonts for use when required.

"Instant Recognition. Everywhere."
